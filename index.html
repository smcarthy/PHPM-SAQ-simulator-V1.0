Public Health & Preventive Medicine SAQ Exam Simulator

Welcome

This simulator approximates the written short‑answer question (SAQ) portion of the Royal College Public Health and Preventive Medicine exam.

What to Expect

This simulation is timed and contains multi‑part questions. Each sub‑question has an assigned point value. Some prompts instruct you to list a specific number of items; each item should be entered separately. Certain prompts may also include “must‑have” points that are required to obtain any credit for that part.

Example: You might be asked to list FIVE steps in the progressive management of an underperforming employee. You must provide FIVE discrete steps, and the rubric includes at least one mandatory item. Another part might ask for TWO immediate recommendations for contaminated well water; failure to mention boiling or disinfecting means zero points even if other suggestions are provided.

Workflow: choose an exam length below, answer the questions in sequence (flag any you want to revisit), review your answers, then submit. You may pause the timer if you need a break, but be aware that the timer runs continuously for the real exam. Upon submission your answers will be automatically downloaded as a file. You can upload this file into the recommended Custom GPT for grading and feedback. Good luck!
