Applied Exam Practice Hub

This page is your compact Royal College PHPM applied-exam practice hub for targeted mock oral rehearsal, station drills, and concise decision-focused prep.

What this page offers

  • Nine topic-based Custom GPT launchers mapped to applied-exam content.
  • Curated challenge scenarios for realistic oral practice.
  • A build-a-station prompt tool for tailored mock oral prep.
  • Concise review tabs on what the RC exam tests and prep for exam day.
  • Suggestions for hot topics and a final-month study planner.

How to use the hub

  • Pick one section button above to focus on one practice mode at a time.
  • Run short, repeated stations and prioritize structure, judgment, and safety.
  • Debrief immediately: what mattered most, what recommendation was safest, and what to improve next.

You do not need to predict the exact station to do well. Residents improve by repeatedly giving clear, safe, decision-focused public health answers across diverse realistic scenarios.
